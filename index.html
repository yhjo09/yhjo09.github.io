I am a Staff Engineer at SAIT (Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology). I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at Yonsei University advised by Prof. Seon Joo Kim.

My research interests focus on Deep Learning and Computer Vision. Particularly, I am interested in deep learning based image/video restoration (super-resolution, deblurring, …) and image quality assessment.

For more information, please refer to my CV.
